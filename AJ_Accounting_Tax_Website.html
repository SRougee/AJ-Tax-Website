Our Story

Two Decades of Expertise, Right Here in Germiston

AJ Accounting & Tax was founded by Alwin Jennings in Elspark, Germiston. With two decades of expertise in taxation and accounting, Alwin has guided countless individuals and businesses through every tax challenge with confidence and precision.

For the past 13 years, our small local practice has proudly served the Germiston community and surrounding areas. We specialise in personal, accurate and affordable service — across a wide range of industries from restaurants and manufacturing to precious metals trading.
